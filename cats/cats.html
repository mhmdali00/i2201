why I should get a cat.

Getting a cat can bring endless joy and companionship to your life. Cats are low-maintenance pets, requiring less time and effort than dogs, yet they offer affection and comfort. Their playful antics and soothing purrs can reduce stress and improve mental health. Cats are also great for smaller living spaces and can adapt well to different lifestyles. Plus, they help keep your home pest-free, making them both adorable and practical companions!.
